# BodySpec Sample Report Analysis

**Source:** https://www.bodyspec.com/sample-report

## Report Sections

### 1. Total Body Composition Overview
- Visual body map (blue=lean, red=fat, white=bone)
- Lean mass distribution charts by region (lbs)
- Total body fat %, total lean mass, total BMC

### 2. Regional Fat Distribution
- Arms (L/R): fat %, lean mass, BMC
- Legs (L/R): fat %, lean mass, BMC
- Trunk: total + android/gynoid subdivisions
- Android:Gynoid ratio
- Historical comparison to baseline and previous scans

### 3. Bone Density
- Total body BMD (g/cm²)
- T-Score and Z-Score
- Skeletal diagram showing bone distribution
- Color-coded reference graphs (normal/at-risk zones)

### 4. Visceral Fat Analysis (VAT)
- Absolute visceral fat mass (lbs/grams)
- Color-coded VAT visualization
- **Percentile ranking** — compared by age/sex against 450,000+ GE iDXA and GE Prodigy scans
- Z-score for metabolic syndrome assessment

### 5. Symmetry / Limb Imbalance
- Left vs right comparison table (fat + lean)
- Imbalance in lbs
- Flags asymmetries >2 lbs

### 6. Trend Tracking
- 6-snapshot shape progression (color-coded body images over time)
- Summary table: total changes in each metric over time
- Regional mass difference graphs
- Baseline vs current comparison

### 7. Data Metrics Summary Table
Columns: Body Fat %, Total Mass, Fat Mass, Lean Mass, BMC
Rows: Arms (L/R), Legs (L/R), Trunk, Android, Gynoid, Totals

## Interactive Features (Digital Dashboard)
- Zoom-in regional analysis (limb-by-limb)
- Color-coded visceral fat maps
- Goal-tracking sliders
- Percentile ranking displays
- **24/7 AI Assistant** — explains metrics, flags anomalies, suggests next steps
- Historical data overlay and comparison
- Progress monitoring over 3-6 month blocks

## Key Differentiators vs Classical DEXA Reports
1. **Interactive** — web dashboard vs static PDF
2. **450K+ comparison dataset** — age/sex-stratified percentiles (most providers have none)
3. **Trend tracking across dozens of scans** — most show 1-3 at best
4. **Symmetry analysis** — not standard on most reports
5. **Android/Gynoid ratio** — metabolic context
6. **VAT-specific analysis** with percentile ranking
7. **AI-powered insights** — conversational agent
8. **Same-day results**
9. **Modern visuals and charts** — color-coded body maps, trend progressions
