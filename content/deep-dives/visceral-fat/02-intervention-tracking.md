### Minimum Detectable Change
- DEXA precision for VAT: ~2-4% CV
- Meaningful change threshold: >10%
- Recommended scan interval: 3-6 months

## Interpreting Trends

<!-- TODO: Write guidance on pattern interpretation -->

### Favorable Patterns
- Decreasing VAT with stable or increasing lean mass
- VAT reduction exceeding total fat loss

### Concerning Patterns
- VAT increasing despite weight loss
- Disproportionate VAT vs subcutaneous

## Clinical Application

<!-- TODO: Add practical workflow -->

### For Providers
1. Baseline VAT establishes risk category

3. Adjust treatment based on response

### For Patients
- Frame VAT as modifiable
- Emphasize lifestyle impact
- Use trends for motivation
